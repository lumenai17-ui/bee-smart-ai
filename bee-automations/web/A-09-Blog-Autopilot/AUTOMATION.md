# Blog Autopilot — Automation Workflow

## Step 1: Research Topics
Use `web-search` to find trending topics for `industry` and `target_audience`:
- Industry news, trends, common questions
- Keyword opportunities with search volume
- Content gaps from competitor analysis

## Step 2: Select and Outline
Use `summarize` to pick the best topic and create an outline:
- Title (SEO-optimized, under 60 chars)
- 5-7 subheadings (H2s)
- Key points per section
- Target keyword and related terms

## Step 3: Generate Article
Use `rewrite` to create the full article (~`word_count` words):
- Engaging introduction with hook
- Well-structured sections with H2/H3
- Practical examples and actionable advice
- Conclusion with CTA

## Step 4: Generate Featured Image
Use `image-generate`: create a professional blog header (1200x630px)

## Step 5: Publish as HTML
Use `web-create` to save as formatted HTML to `output_dir`

## Success Criteria
- Article is factually accurate and well-structured
- SEO optimized with target keyword
- Ready for publishing in the configured format
